# ArtistPass Outreach Playbook

This is the first-week outreach plan. The goal is not mass spam. The goal is 20-30 real conversations and 2 paid pilots.

## Target Lists

Create 100 targets:

- 25 actor/headshot photographers.
- 25 acting coaches/classes/workshops.
- 25 reel editors/showreel editors/video editors.
- 25 actors/singers/models/anchors/creators.

Use public business/contact pages, Instagram bios, LinkedIn and warm introductions.

## Qualification

Prioritize people who already sell or use:

- headshots;
- showreels;
- acting portfolios;
- casting profiles;
- performer websites;
- artist EPKs;
- creator portfolios.

Avoid:

- people asking for free speculative work;
- people with no usable assets;
- people who want private video hosting as the first requirement.

After 3-4 days:

Track:

- target type;
- name;

- message date;
- response;
- call booked;
