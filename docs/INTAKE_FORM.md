## Pilot Qualification

Accept as pilot if:

- They have at least 3 usable photos.
- They have at least 1 reel/video/work link.
- They can approve copy and assets within 48 hours.
- They understand videos remain link-based for now.
- They accept that the first offer is a setup service, not a full SaaS account system.

Do not accept as pilot if:

- They need private video hosting/passcodes immediately.
- They expect unlimited custom design.
- They do not have permission to use supplied photos/videos.
- They want speculative work without paying.
