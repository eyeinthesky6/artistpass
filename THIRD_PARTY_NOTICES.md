## Runtime Libraries And Fonts

ArtistPass uses these third-party projects under their own licenses:

- [Vercel Blob](https://github.com/vercel/storage) for optional browser-admin uploads and live configuration.
- [React](https://github.com/facebook/react), [React DOM](https://github.com/facebook/react) and [Babel Standalone](https://github.com/babel/babel), loaded at runtime by the existing page renderer.
- [qrcode-generator](https://github.com/kazuhikoarase/qrcode-generator), [html2canvas](https://github.com/niklasvh/html2canvas) and [jsPDF](https://github.com/parallax/jsPDF) for QR codes and browser-generated image/PDF downloads.
- [Instrument Serif](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Instrument+Serif) and [Hanken Grotesk](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Hanken+Grotesk), loaded from Google Fonts.

The repository does not relicense those projects. Their upstream licenses and terms continue to apply.
