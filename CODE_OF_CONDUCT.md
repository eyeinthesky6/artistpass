# ArtistPass Code of Conduct

ArtistPass should be a welcoming place for artists, developers, designers and first-time open-source contributors.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful, patient and specific.
- Give feedback about the work, not the person.
- Welcome questions from non-technical contributors.
- Protect people's privacy and use fictional or approved sample material only.
- Credit other people's work and respect licenses.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, threats, discrimination or personal attacks.
- Publishing someone's private information or media without permission.
- Sexualised or demeaning comments or imagery.
- Spam, scams or repeated off-topic promotion.
- Deliberately disrupting project discussions or releases.

## Reporting A Problem

Email `6edroid@gmail.com` with the subject `ArtistPass conduct report`. Include links or screenshots if useful. Reports will be reviewed privately and handled as fairly and promptly as possible.

Project maintainers may edit or remove comments, commits, issues, pull requests or other contributions that break these rules. They may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary.

## Scope

This code applies in the ArtistPass repository and in public spaces where someone is representing ArtistPass.
