Two honest paths

You shouldn't need to become a developer to own your portfolio.

01

I'm an artist

Ask for assisted setup. A developer deploys the site once; after that, you update your words, photos and PDFs from the browser Admin.

Ask for assisted setup Best for non-technical artists, managers and collaborators.
02

I'm a developer

Clone the open-source template, deploy it on Vercel, choose an Admin password and hand over a working site—without running a heavy CMS.

Deploy your own copy Best for developers, studios and technically comfortable creators.
